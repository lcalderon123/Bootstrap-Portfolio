Dream #1:
Educator

Sometimes the most fragile people can be the best at breaking you. Six years of using every particle of my strength to walk through the door every day just to leave a deflated mess no longer was worth my struggle. I met some magical children along the way, but unfortunately the boundaries created to help them succeed only held them back. It became too much of a burden to have to uphold the rules while knowing it often did more damage than good. I walked away.

Sorta-Dream #2
Business Woman

If you cant break the rules you might as well make them. For the past three years I've been trying to climb up the oiled down ladder of a fortune 500 company. I've come a long way and am now regarded by my peers as the walking source of their necessary knowledge. Yet, every attempt I've made at taking the next step in my professional evolution I've been brushed aside. I've been told I'm the best, I've been told I'm an example, I'm asked to help mold the new people that walk through our doors. Yet I'm not good enough, but I will be for my next chapter.

Dream #3:
Creator

There's a dream waiting to be found under every unturned stone. Fifteen years ago I took a class in high school for webpage design. I loved it so much but was too young to be open to what possibilities it might have held for me. A few years ago I met someone who inspired me in so many ways and exposed me to new loves I never knew I would have. They took my hand and walked me back to the path that high school girl had stumbled away from. I wont get lost this time.
